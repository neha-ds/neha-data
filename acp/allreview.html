Lovely Relaxed Site

We’ve just spent a couple of days at ACP. We didn’t know what to expect and the rough driveway leading to the site left us wondering. What a pleasant surprise we had as we entered the main camping area. The large first field was very welcoming. We were lucky as it wasn’t too busy so we had plenty of choice for a pitch and we’re able to hook up to an electric point (£1, £2 coins required). We explored the other fields but were quite happy with our choice of pitch. The showers, toilets, washing up point were all excellent. The site is situated in a beautiful valley and it was so enjoyable to experience such a peaceful environment even when a large group of D of E young people arrived, they were very respectful and set off the rolling morning in heavy rain yet all good humoured. Alfreston is a beautiful village, certainly worth spending time exploring, the site is close to the village and there are numerous walks in the area. The ACP staff were so friendly and helpful. We’ve just had a lovely break and will certainly be back.

Sam Mc
01 SEPTEMBER 2020, 08:59:06
Awesome Camping Trip

We have just returned from a fabulous camping trip with our friends. There were 12 of us in total (3 families) and I know I can speak on behalf of them all to say we had a great time at Alfriston Camp Site, Kev was a fantastic host, when we arrived we found a spot and he popped over to say hello, point out the facilities, we paid and he gave us a band to attach to the tent so knew when we were paid up until - great system! When we arrived on the Thursday it was very quiet with lots of space, but by the weekend that changed a lot and the site was packed! There were two cubicles with toilets, sometimes a queue but not ever for longer than 5-10 mins. The toilets and showers were both kept clean throughout the day, another camper on site had become unwell (think they'd had a few too many and were sick) but this was cleaned up really quickly, they are tirelessly making sure that everyone is ok, checking if we need logs and kindling for fires (and when we ran out and couldn't see them we made a quick phone call and within minutes someone arrived with some wood!) Cannot fault the staff for attentiveness! Was great to see some solar panels, and the staff go through the rubbish and recycle everything they which is above and beyond in my opinion - not a fun job! We went on a great hike, visited a couple of the local pubs, went swimming in Cuckmere Meanders and the kids spent lots of time on the multiple rope swings! Fantastic! Would definitely visit again!
